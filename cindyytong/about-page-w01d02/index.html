Cindy Y. Tong

Hi there! I’m Cindy Tong: a born and raised New Yorker who just moved out to the West Coast. I spend my days stumbling on new challenges and living fully.

After graduating from NYU’s College of Arts and Sciences with an accelerated three-year Bachelor’s degree in Economics and Art History, I considered Law School while I worked on the Structured Products division of law firm, Davis Polk and Wardwell. Our primary clients included Deutsche Bank, Morgan Stanley and Citigroup.

To satisfy my penchant for NYC property voyeurism, I became a licensed real estate salesperson and the Business and Operations Manager for the McDonough Hershkowitz Team at Core. With over $800 million in NYC residential sales under our belt, we were recognized in 2015 by Forbes as the 25th highest grossing team in NYC.
