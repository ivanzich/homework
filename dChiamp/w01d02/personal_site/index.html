About Me

I'm kind of a design nerd. I am happiest when I am making things from scratch or customizing existing products, and I am not satisfied until I've made something more functional and personal.

I studied Urban Design and Planning at Occidental College, which deeply informed my aesthetic and approach to life and art. I believe that a strong community is essential for success. Learning from and teaching each other new skills is the best way to create the world we want to live in.

Whether I am coding, advocating for more bicycle friendly streets, making a delicious vegan meal, or working on my motorcycle, I focus on sustainable and functional design to create the changes I want to see.
